About Me

Devesh Patil

Quality Engineer

My job contains....

  • Continuous monitoring standard compliance and initiate corrective and preventive actions in case of deviation observed Establishing and maintaining quality plans.

  • Inward goods inspection (IGI) of Raw materials. In addition, carrying out vendor process audits for improvement of product quality at vendors site as per company requirement.

  • Monitoring and analyzing process data to drive improvements by process capability in Product quality reducing rejections and reworks in continuous casting rolling mill and conductor stranding sections.

  • Analyzing chemical compositions of 1050, 1060, 6201, grade aluminum.

  • Ensuring all testing facility like Optical Emission Spectrometer (OES) analysis for percentage of composition of elements in aluminum samples & Ingot samples, Tensile Testing machine & Micro ohm meter for mechanical and electrical test on rod and wire samples.

  • Ensuring and controlling manufacturing process like Heat treatment (Ageing/annealing), Die drafting and draw met oil viscosity, visual checks in machines. For ensuring FTR.

  • Conduct process Audit at conductor and continuous casting rolling mill sections. Also monitoring product in-process parameters. Reducing rejection and rework.

  • Ensuring product parameters from RM to final Finished good stage. Monitoring and maintaining process parameter records especially for New Product Development. To handle the New Product Parameter.

  • Implementation of SOPs and guidelines of process as per company guideline.

  • Preparing and maintaining Product test reports, FTR reports and Process capability reports. In process Quality and inwards goods quality clearance in SAP to ensure OTD (On- time Delivery) of Finished goods with respect to sales target.

  • Active part in ISO 9001-2015, ISO 14001-2015, OHSAS 18001-2007, NABL and internal Audits.
